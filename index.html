Tic Tac Toe

Tic tac toe, also known as noughts and crosses by our neighbors across the pond, was originally a simple game played by hand due to how simple the game is. The goal of the game is to attain 3 of your marks, whether it be X or O, in a straight line. That means the line can be either horizontally, vertically, or even diagonally. With the advent of the digital age, we have this long lived game right at our fingertips, so good luck and have fun!
